Tutora

Educational Management Platform

Project Overview

Purpose: To automate student and staff workflows, significantly reducing administrative overhead by 35%.

How it works: A specialized educational management platform that handles complex user management and administrative tasks securely.

Key Achievements & Contributions

Architected and delivered the comprehensive education platform as a freelance project.

  • Workflow Automation: Automated student and staff workflows to drastically reduce manual administrative tasks.
  • Secure Architectures: Designed comprehensive user management architectures tailored to strict client specifications.
  • Access Control: Implemented secure Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) portals for different user roles (students, staff, admins).
  • System Design: Built from the ground up to ensure high data integrity and workflow efficiency.
